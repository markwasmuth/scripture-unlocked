Genesis — King James Version (KJV)
The Beginning — From Judgment to Restoration
This chapter reveals two distinct creations: the original perfect creation of verse 1, and the restoration that begins in verse 2 after the destruction of the first earth age. Understanding the gap between these two verses unlocks the entire plan of God from Genesis to Revelation.
Gen 1:1
In the beginning God created the heaven and the earth.
Teaching
Strong's (3)
Cross-Refs (5)
Narrator
Here we find the foundational truth that establishes everything else in Scripture. There are two bodies mentioned — heaven and earth — and God created both. The verse simply states a fact and leaves the time factor completely open. It does not say when, but reveals the eternal span of time and the creative power of our Almighty God. This original creation was perfect, as we learn from Isaiah chapter 45 verse 18, where God declares He created the earth not in vain but to be inhabited. The Hebrew word bara means to create out of nothing, which only God can do. This verse establishes the first earth age when everything was in perfect order before Satan's rebellion.
