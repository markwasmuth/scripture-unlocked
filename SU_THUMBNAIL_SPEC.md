# Scripture Unlocked — Static Study Thumbnail Spec

## Purpose
Create a repeatable YouTube-friendly thumbnail/template for **chapter-by-chapter** and **verse-by-verse** Bible studies.

Default format:
- **static image**
- **voice/audio-led teaching**
- **repeatable premium template**
- HeyGen/avatar reserved for promos, intros, and special flagship pieces

---

## Core Direction
The thumbnail should feel:
- reverent
- calm
- premium
- authoritative
- biblical
- clean
- not flashy
- not cluttered
- not generic AI fantasy
- not church-flyer busy

Emotional lane:
**"Come sit down, open the Word, and see."**

---

## Layout Structure
Use a **locked, repeatable template**.

### 3-part structure
1. **Top strip**
   - small brand line
   - text: `Scripture Unlocked`

2. **Main title area**
   - hero text = **Book + Chapter**
   - examples:
     - `REVELATION 1`
     - `GENESIS 12`
     - `JOHN 3`

3. **Subtitle area**
   - smaller supporting line
   - examples:
     - `Verse-by-Verse Bible Study`
     - `Chapter-by-Chapter Study`

Optional:
- small bottom badge: `KJV` or `Bible Study`

---

## Visual Composition
Recommended split:
- **60–65%** visual scene/background
- **35–40%** title-safe text area

Keep text inside a safe central box with generous margins.
Do not place text on top of busy detail.

---

## Visual Style
Use:
- dark rich background
- parchment / open Bible / ancient texture
- warm gold / amber light
- subtle symbolic imagery only
- strong contrast for mobile readability

Good symbolic elements:
- open Bible
- parchment
- lamp/light
- scroll
- temple stone
- olive wood
- desert atmosphere

Avoid:
- fantasy-style AI art
- too many elements competing
- floating text over busy scenes
- tiny verse references as hero text
- loud effects / over-cinematic clutter

---

## Typography Rules
- **Book + Chapter is the hero**
- subtitle is secondary
- brand strip is smallest
- no more than **3 text blocks total**
- high contrast only
- big bold readable type
- phone readability is non-negotiable

Priority order:
1. Book + Chapter
2. Study type subtitle
3. Scripture Unlocked brand line

---

## YouTube Performance Rules
This is a study series, not clickbait content.
So:
- do **not** make the hook bigger than the chapter reference
- do make the **Book + Chapter** instantly readable
- optimize for recognition, trust, and repeatability
- the channel should feel organized like a premium study library

---

## Default Template Example
Top:
`SCRIPTURE UNLOCKED`

Main:
`REVELATION 7`

Sub:
`Verse-by-Verse Bible Study`

Optional badge:
`KJV`

---

## Use Cases
### Default
- chapter studies
- verse-by-verse studies
- long-form Bible teaching

### Not default
Use HeyGen/avatar only for:
- promos
- intros
- welcome videos
- testimony-style videos
- flagship doctrine pieces

---

## Success Test
The template is correct if it feels:
- like a serious Bible study series
- recognizable at a glance
- easy to repeat for dozens of chapters
- premium enough for YouTube
- readable on a phone in under 1 second
